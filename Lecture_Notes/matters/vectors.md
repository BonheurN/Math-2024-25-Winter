## 9. Linear equations by Gauss Elimination

$$\begin{cases}
x + 2y - 2z = 4 \\
2x + y + z = 0 \\
3x + 2y + z = 1
\end{cases}
\quad
\begin{cases}
x + y + z - t = 2 \\
2x + y + z = 3 \\
-x + z - t = 0 \\
3x + 2y - z + 2t = -1
\end{cases}
\quad
\begin{cases}
x + y - z - t = 0 \\
2x + 3y - 2z + t = 4 \\
3x + 5z = 0 \\
-x + y - 3z + 2t = 3
\end{cases}
$$
